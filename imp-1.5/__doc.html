Loads assets from Collada 1.4.1 and 1.5 XML documents into the data structures provided by the go-collada/dom package. Note, Collada 1.4.1 documents are insta-bootstrapped to version 1.5 in-memory using the go-collada/conv-1.4.1-to-1.5 package.
